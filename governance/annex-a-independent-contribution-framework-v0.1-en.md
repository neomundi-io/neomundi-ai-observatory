# Annex A — Independent Contribution Framework

## NeoMundi AI Observatory

**Version: 0.1 — Draft submitted for review**
**Status: Governance document under consultation**
**Associated primary document: NeoMundi Spirit & Contribution Framework — v1.0**

> **Courtesy translation.** This English version is provided for accessibility and international participation. In the event of any discrepancy, the French version shall prevail.

This Annex supplements the document *“NeoMundi Spirit & Contribution Framework.”*

It sets out the practical terms applicable to individuals recognised as contributors, reviewers, methodological peers, experts, or infrastructure contributors to the NeoMundi AI Observatory.

## 1. Status of the Contribution

Contributions are made on a voluntary, independent, and non-exclusive basis.

This Annex does not create an employment contract, a relationship of subordination, an obligation of attendance, an obligation of availability, an obligation to achieve results, a mandate of representation, or any promise of future remuneration.

Each contributor remains free to determine the pace, form, and scope of their participation, within the agreed contribution perimeter.

## 2. Scope of Contribution

Contributors may participate in one or more areas identified with NeoMundi Research, including methodology, research, auditability, data, infrastructure, compliance, law, governance, security, evidence, scientific communication, or review.

All contributions remain voluntary and proportionate to the contributor’s availability.

## 3. Independence and Non-Exclusivity

Contributors retain full freedom to continue their professional, academic, entrepreneurial, associative, or research activities.

The status of NeoMundi contributor creates no exclusivity and no general restriction on collaboration with other organisations.

## 4. Attribution, Rights, and Use of Work

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, contributors retain their rights in their original work, including texts, analyses, reports, code, visualisations, protocols, methods, or any other formalised contribution.

NeoMundi Research may cite, reference, archive, publish, or integrate a contribution into its work only within a framework agreed with its author and with appropriate attribution.

Any substantial modification to a signed text, or any co-publication, requires prior agreement with its author.

## 5. Confidentiality

Contributors undertake not to disclose non-public information to which they may gain access through their contribution, including internal data, mappings, technical elements, strategic documents, partner information, or unpublished results.

Where the nature of the access justifies it, NeoMundi Research may propose a separate confidentiality agreement.

## 6. Conflicts of Interest

Contributors are invited to disclose in good faith any situation that may create an actual or apparent conflict of interest concerning a matter addressed within the Observatory.

This disclosure is intended solely to preserve the integrity, transparency, and methodological credibility of the work. It does not prevent participation.

## 7. No Authority to Represent

Contributors may not bind NeoMundi Research, speak officially on its behalf, use its name or brand, enter into agreements, or present a position as institutional without prior written authorisation.

Likewise, NeoMundi Research will not present a contributor as an official representative without their consent.

## 8. Ending a Contribution

Contributors may end their participation at any time, without having to provide justification.

NeoMundi Research may suspend or end a contribution in the event of a confidentiality issue, an undisclosed conflict of interest, harm to methodological integrity, legal risk, or a clear misalignment with the purpose of the Observatory.

The end of a contribution does not affect attribution already granted, nor any confidentiality obligations applicable to non-public information previously received.

## 9. Applicable Law

This Annex is attached to the contribution framework of NeoMundi Research, an initiative carried by a French association.

It is governed by French law.
